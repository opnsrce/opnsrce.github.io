If you&#8217;re looking for an interactive way to mess around with Ruby without installing anything, I&#8217;d checkout [TryRuby.org](http://www.tryruby.org/). It&#8217;s a really cool interactive terminal that let&#8217;s you execute ruby commands from the comfort of your browser. You can even step through a quick 15 minute tutorial inspired by [Why&#8217;s Guide to Ruby](http://www.rubyinside.com/media/poignant-guide.pdf).
